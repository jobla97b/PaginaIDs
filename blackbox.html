Black Box

A black box will be made with the objective of knowing what you want to obtain and the inputs to achieve it.

Posted by Joseph Blanco on April 11, 2019

Enter the entrances and exits

Description of TICKETS:

Teachers trained to teach: Teachers with the mastery that is needed of the classes they will teach and ability to teach students efficiently their knowledge about the class.

Complete study plan for the education of the students that has to be executed by the public and private schools: Structuring of the study plan with all the necessary subjects and correctly organized for the use of the schools.

Universities disclose the student profile they want in their careers: Universities must give more public information about the student profile they need through web pages, social networks; all means of information that serves to make students more easily aware of this important information.

Description of DEPARTURES:

The teacher makes a better transmission of education to students: Students better understand the topics taught by the teacher.

The student receives all the necessary subjects for their education in Secondary: The student will no longer have problems with the syllabus that he receives from a University because the study plan they received in high school was developed with the objective of satisfying the needs they have to enter any public university.

The student will have a better vision of which university and career to choose: You will know the requirements that you must have as a student in public Universities with their respective careers.
