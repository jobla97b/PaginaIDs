Studian Profile

The objective of this page is to inform the students of the different profiles in the public universities of Nicaragua

Posted by Joseph Blanco on April 11, 2019

This project aims to contribute to the problem that high school students have when they want to enter public universities. Some of the causes of this education problem are related to the precarious economic and infrastructure conditions of families and schools, the ignorance of the importance of learning for their good education, the lack of importance of both students and parents and teachers in the interest and importance of passing a level, not of the true knowledge acquired.

In this way, facilitating the profiles of the students that the universities want and knowing that they are facing a little beyond the basic knowledge of the Nicaraguan society, this will help them to make a desicion a little more solid than what they decided to study.
