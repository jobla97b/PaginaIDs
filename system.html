Selection the best objective in this project

Posted by Joseph Blanco on April 11, 2019

Objectives and alternatives selected as well as their respective consequences.

O6 Improve attention to learning problems in schools at least 60% from the first evaluation cut until the last day of classes (February 20, 2020 to November 26, 2021).

Improve attention to learning problems: Increase the number of students with learning problems attended in schools.


O7 Improve school infrastructure from 40% to 60% at least for better motivation for both staff and students starting on November 30, 2021 through February 22, 2022.

Infrastructure: Classrooms, cafeterias, laboratories, recreational centers, etc.


O8 Teach students values ​​and good habits from home at a minimum of 50% from April 17, 2020 until April 14, 2023.

Values: Honesty, Generosity, Respect, etc.

Good habits: In the study and at work

O9. Improve the Assessment System of admission exams in public universities from January 5, 2021 to February 20, 2021.

O10 Increase the number of Secondary students who know the student profile of the Universities by at least 90% beginning on January 17, 2020 until the end of the school year.

O11 Reduce the number of secondary students who classify public universities for non-educational interests up to 20% from January 3, 2022 to January 15, 2022.

Non-educational interests: Economic, political, family and friendship interests.

O12 Reduce the number of Secondary students advancing for non-educational interests by up to 50% from February 15, 2020 until January 4, 2021.

Non-educational interests: Economic, political, family and friendship interests.

Alternatives chosen:

06 A1: Implement reinforcement classes for students who have difficulties in their subjects.

0.7 A3: Implement an adequate maintenance and conditioning plan for the infrastructures.

08 A1: Carry out training programs for parents on family education for their children.

09 A1: Conduct an examination of classes that influence with great potential the careers of the universities (50%), a psychometric exam where it is evaluated if it is apt for the offered careers (30%) and 4th and 5th grades (20%) .

010. A1: Provide information about the student profile through social networks.

011. A2: Perform audits that control alterations in the results of the admission exams in the different universities.

012 A2: Conduct lectures to teachers to raise awareness of the responsibility they have as educators.
